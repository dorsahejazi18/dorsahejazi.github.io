My Journey

Bridging Logic and Creativity

My path in civil engineering began with a childhood dream of building my own house. Later, the simple observation that water is the most essential element connecting a community led me to pursue my Masters degree, where I chose to dive deep into the complexities of water science. For me, civil engineering isn't just about formulas; its about the balance of logic and creativity. I find that my life outside of work feeds into my professional identity: Art teaches me to slow down and view complex problems from different perspectives, noticing the fine details that others might miss. Fitness provides the discipline and positive mindset necessary to tackle the long-term challenges of infrastructure and design. Today, I am eager to apply my specialized knowledge to real-world projects that make a tangible impact on the environment and the communities we serve.
